Health Factor Over Time
Withdrawal: W_max = C_user × [ L − (L−1) / LTV ] — max collateral removable while HF ≥ 1.
Unlooping: Each step: withdraw max → swap → repay debt. Repeat until B = 0. Higher LTV = larger withdrawal per step = fewer loops to exit.
Time to liq: T = −365 × ln(HF₀) / ln(decay) where decay = (1 + r_stake − r_siphon) / (1 + r_borrow).
stETH-ETH e-mode defaults: Aave borrow LTV 93%, Twyne boosted borrow LTV 97%.
